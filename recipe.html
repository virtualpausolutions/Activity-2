Sinigang na Baboy

A traditional Filipino cuisine

Ingredients:

  • 1kg of Pork
  • Tomatoes
  • Kangkong
  • Okra
  • Talong
  • Siling Pansigang
  • Onion
  • 1 ltr of water
  • Sinigang powder
  • Pork Cubes
  • Patis

  1. Boil the 1kg of meat in a 500mg of water for 15 minutes
  2. Put 2 tbsp of patis
  3. Put the pork cubes
  4. Add the sliced tomatoes and onions
  5. Boil for another 15 minutes
  6. add the remaining water
  7. add talong
  8. add siling pansigang
  9. add okra
  10. pour the sinigang mix powder
  11. and add the kangkong and cook for 3 minutes
